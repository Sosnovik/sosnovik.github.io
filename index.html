On Detecting Policy-Related Political Ads: An Exploratory Analysis of Meta Ads during the 2022 French Election

Vera Sosnovik, Romaissa Kessi, Maximin Coavoux and Oana Goga

WWW, 2023

We focus on political ads that are related to the policy during the 2022 French presidential elections. This paper proposes automated methods based on pre-trained models to classify ads in 14 main policy groups identified by the Comparative Agenda Project (CAP).

Understanding the Complexity of Detecting Political Ads

Vera Sosnovik and Oana Goga

WWW, 2021

We investigate whether we can reliably distinguish political ads from non-political ads. We take an empirical approach to analyze what kind of ads are deemed political by ordinary people and what kind of ads lead to disagreement.
